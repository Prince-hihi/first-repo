Air Pollution Problems

Health Impacts

Air pollution causes respiratory diseases, cardiovascular issues, and poses significant risks, especially to children and the elderly.

Environmental Damage

Pollutants harm ecosystems and contribute to issues like acid rain and loss of biodiversity.

Climate Change

Increased emissions lead to climate change, causing extreme weather and affecting agriculture and livelihoods.

Ways to Control Air Pollution

Stricter Emissions Regulations

Enforcing regulations can significantly reduce harmful pollutants.

Adoption of Renewable Energy

Shifting to renewable energy sources decreases reliance on fossil fuels.

Community Education and Awareness

Educating communities fosters public involvement in pollution reduction efforts.
